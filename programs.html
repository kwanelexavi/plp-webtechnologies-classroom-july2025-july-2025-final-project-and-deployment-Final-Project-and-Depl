Our Programs

Holistic Support for Every Child

From homework clubs to health clinics, our programmes respond to the real needs of Soweto’s children. Explore how we develop confident learners, leaders, and change-makers.

View Focus Areas
Education Support

Academic Excellence, One Learner at a Time

Daily after-school tutoring covers maths, science, languages, and exam preparation. Learners access curated study materials, mentorship, and a quiet environment to concentrate on learning.

Homework Club

Volunteer teachers provide one-on-one support tailored to each learner’s school curriculum.

Digital Lab

Computer skills, coding workshops, and online research sessions prepare youth for modern careers.

Matric Mastery

Weekend masterclasses with qualified teachers push Grade 12 learners toward university entrance scores.

Sports & Wellness

Healthy Bodies, Confident Minds

Sport is a powerful teacher. Our sports division keeps children active, reduces school dropouts, and promotes teamwork, discipline, and mental wellness.

Soccer Academy

Mixed-gender teams practice thrice a week, compete regionally, and receive training gear and nutrition.

Run Soweto

Community running club promotes cardiovascular health, family participation, and mental resilience.

Wellness Coaching

Partner physiotherapists and psychologists guide injury prevention, counselling, and mindfulness.

Creative Arts

Celebrating Culture & Storytelling

We nurture imagination through music, dance, theatre, and visual arts. Children explore self-expression, learn heritage, and build confidence performing for audiences.

Voices of Vilakazi

Choir, spoken word, and storytelling nights celebrate African languages and lived experiences.

Art Studio

Fine arts, photography, and design classes build portfolios and open doors to bursary opportunities.

Community Theatre

Original productions tour schools and festivals, addressing social topics like bullying and gender equality.

Family & Community

Supporting the Whole Household

Our wraparound services ensure families remain stable. We partner with social workers, clinics, and local businesses to address food security, safety, and income generation.

Nutrition Kitchen

Daily meals and food parcels for vulnerable households, plus cooking workshops promoting healthy diets.

Family Support Desk

Social workers offer counselling, legal referrals, and home visits to ensure child protection.

Future Leaders Club

Teen leadership training, career guidance, and job shadowing experiences prepare youth for the workplace.

Community Events

Holiday camps, reading festivals, and street clean-ups bring the whole neighbourhood together.

Safeguarding

Safe Spaces for Every Child

We operate with rigorous safeguarding standards. All staff and volunteers undergo background checks, child protection training, and ongoing supervision. Parents sign consent forms and receive regular updates.

Key Policies

  • Child protection and reporting protocol
  • Gender equity and inclusive participation policy
  • Code of conduct for staff and volunteers
  • Emergency response and health guidelines
