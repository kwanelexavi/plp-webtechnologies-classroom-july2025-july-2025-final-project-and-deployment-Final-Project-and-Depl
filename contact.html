Finding the Ubuntu Learning Hub

Located in the heart of Orlando West, our centre is easily accessible via public transport. Street parking is available near Vilakazi Street. For group visits, please notify us in advance.

  • Gautrain bus stop — 5-minute walk
  • Rea Vaya station — 8-minute walk
  • Uber and Bolt drop-off zone on Vilakazi Street

Hub Directions

Use GPS: -26.2378, 27.9115

Entrance opposite the Hector Pieterson Museum. Look for the mural of children holding books.
